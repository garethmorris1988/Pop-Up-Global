Pop Up Global is a production house specialising in original live entertainment. With teams in the UK and UAE, we create unforgettable experiences across cruise, leisure, retail and events, on land and at sea.

Where We Work

Five Sectors.
One Standard.

We operate across leisure, retail, cruise, events and consultancy. Our productions are built to run night after night, engineered for lasting impact on land and at sea.

Sector 01

Leisure

Theme parks, visitor attractions and leisure destinations. Entertainment programmes that drive footfall, dwell time and return visits.

Sector 02

Retail

Shopping centres and retail destinations. Seasonal programmes and year-round entertainment that make your venue a destination in its own right.

Sector 03

Cruise

West End-standard productions designed for life at sea. Shows built for repeat audiences and the unique demands of the cruise environment.

Sector 04
