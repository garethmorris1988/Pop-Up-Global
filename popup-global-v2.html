Pop Up Global is a boutique production house specialising in world-class, original live entertainment. With teams in the UK and UAE, we create unforgettable experiences across cruise, leisure, retail and one-off events — both on land and at sea.

At the heart of everything we do is storytelling. We don't just put on a show — we create moments that stop people in their tracks, connect with your audience, and make your venue impossible to forget.
